Hello there,

Welcome to the official "Project Wedding Dance" website. I'm Petra Valcsev, the brain behind this initiative.

My project is all about creating magical and unforgettable first dances for couples on their big day. I understand how daunting it can be to dance in front of an audience and your loved ones. That's why I had the idea to turn this potentially nerve-wracking experience into something truly exciting.

My own dance journey started when I was just three years old. I specialized in Latin dances and received training in all the classic dance styles. Along the way, I've had some fantastic moments, like winning the national championship in Latin dances three times in Hungary, and making it into the Austrian Erste Bundesliga in 2023.

I believe my experience will help you to transform this stressful moment of yours into something very special and unforgettable.

Our approach

Step #1 - Let's Get Started

Are you getting married and want to nail that first dance? Just send me a message, and we'll set up a chat where we can talk about your song choice and any wedding ideas you have.

Step #2 - Time to Dance

Once I know your song and ideas, I'll create a special dance routine for you, which usually takes about 2 weeks. We can then schedule your first dance lesson whenever it's convenient for you, and the fun begins!

Step #3 - Learn the Moves

Learning a 3-minute dance usually takes about 5-7 sessions. I'll show you the dance steps, and we'll record videos at the end of each session to help you remember. Usually I prepare different variations to the same song to make sure you feel super confident dancing the choreography.
